This event is a week-long Bhagwat Katha led by Raseshwari Devi Ji, along with Aarti, Kirtan and Manasadhyan meditation. It is an opportunity for spiritual seekers to immerse themselves in divine knowledge and the eternal wisdom of the Shrimad Bhagwatam.
The event runs from 13th to 19th December 2025, daily from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM at Meiwar Banquet, Down Town Mall, Kolkata.
The event is open to all — devotees, spiritual seekers, families and those simply curious about the wisdom of the Bhagwatam. Entry is completely free of charge.
You can reach out via the Contact page, or follow us on our social media channels for regular updates. Volunteer opportunities are available — register and indicate your interest in supporting the event.
